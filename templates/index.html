üçØ Bitcoin Tipping Jars

Create beautiful, shareable Bitcoin tipping jars that make it easy for anyone to send you Bitcoin or Lightning payments with just a quick scan.

Why I Built This

I created this site because I wanted to share my Bitcoin books for free with others while giving readers a simple way to tip me. There are two reasons this matters to me: it's fun to get a small payment when you see others have read your book, and making your first Bitcoin payment is a great first step in any Bitcoin journey.

But I realized this concept could work for so much more than just books! That's why I kept the Bitcoin tipping jar generic - it can be used for any content, service, or interaction where you want to give people an easy way to show appreciation with Bitcoin.

üí° Why QR Codes Link to This Website

You might wonder: why not just create direct payment QR codes? Great question! Here's why our approach is better:

  • Flexibility: Change your payment addresses anytime without reprinting QR codes
  • Referral codes: Help new users get started with Bitcoin by including exchange referral codes
  • Personal touch: Users can leave thank you messages instead of just sending anonymous payments
  • Future-proof: Add new payment methods or features without updating your QR codes

This makes the tipping experience more personal, flexible, and educational for everyone involved!
